UltraXpert Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to UltraXpert. Your privacy is paramount to us. UltraXpert is an advanced iOS application designed for medical professionals, offering AI-powered ultrasound scan enhancement, patient data management, and diagnostic reporting tools.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services effectively, we collect the following types of information:

  • Professional Credentials: When you sign up, we collect your Name, Email Address, and Medical License ID to verify your professional status.
  • Patient Information: Our app allows you to store patient names, ages, medical history, and clinical notes. This data is handled with the highest level of security.
  • Medical Images and Scans: We process ultrasound scans and medical images you upload for the purpose of AI enhancement and diagnostic reporting.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with the app, such as features used, time spent, and tool performance metrics.

3. Artificial Intelligence and Data Safety

UltraXpert utilizes sophisticated Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence models to enhance scan quality (improving SNR, sharpness, and contrast).

  • Processing: AI processing is conducted securely. We may use anonymized image data to further train and improve our AI models if you have explicitly opted into our "AI Improvement Program."
  • Diagnostic Support: Our AI features are intended to assist medical professionals and should not be used as the sole basis for clinical decisions.
  • Data Anonymization: When used for AI training, all patient-identifiable information (PII) is removed to ensure absolute anonymity.

4. Security and Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to ensure compliance with medical data standards, including:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.
  • Storage at Rest: Sensitive patient data and images are encrypted using AES-256 standard on our secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Access Control: Only authorized users can access the data they have uploaded. We do not share data with third parties without your explicit consent.
  • HIPAA Alignment: Our architecture is designed to align with HIPAA guidelines for the protection of Personal Health Information (PHI).

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data within UltraXpert:

  • Data Access: You can view all patient records and reports you've created at any time.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete specific patient records or your entire account through the settings menu. Deleting your account will remove all associated data from our servers.
  • Analytics Opt-out: You can enable or disable usage analytics and crash reporting in the "Security & Privacy" settings.
